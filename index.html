Who We Are

A practice built on trust, not volume.

Foundation Dental Center was created to give this community something different: a local, accountable, clinically serious dental home that does not feel corporate. A place built for patients who need real answers and a trusted path forward.

Every case is managed by Dr. Pasicznyk from the first consultation to the final restoration. Your plan reflects your anatomy, your goals, and your timeline — not a protocol.

Is This for Me?

You may be closer to a solution than you think.

Many patients wait years before asking for help because they are embarrassed, overwhelmed, or have been told their situation is too complicated. If any of this sounds familiar, a consultation may help you understand what is truly possible.

  • You have missing, failing, or loose teeth.
  • You struggle with loose or uncomfortable dentures.
  • You struggle to eat the foods you want.
  • You have been told you are not a candidate for implants.
  • You have been told you do not have enough bone for implants.
  • You want a second opinion before committing to major treatment.
  • You feel overwhelmed by conflicting treatment recommendations.
  • You want a clear plan, not a rushed sales consultation.

Our Philosophy

Results that are planned, not promised.

Every patient's situation is different, and every result begins with diagnosis. We focus on understanding the foundation first — bone, bite, function, esthetics, health history, and long-term maintenance — so your plan is realistic, durable, and designed around you.

Our goal is not just a better smile in photos. It is helping you eat more comfortably, smile more confidently, and understand the path forward.
